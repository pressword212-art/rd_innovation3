The organization focuses on providing emergency relief, sustainable development, and addressing the root causes of poverty. Maldives Islamic Tourism

Islamic tourism in the Maldives offers a unique blend of spiritual and leisure experiences, making it an ideal destination for Muslim travelers. With a Muslim-majority population and a rich Islamic heritage, the Maldives provides numerous halal-friendly amenities, including halal-certified dining options, prayer facilities, and private accommodations. Many resorts cater to Muslim guests by offering alcohol-free environments, separate swimming areas for women, and dedicated prayer spaces. The country's commitment to preserving its Islamic culture is evident in its tourism offerings, ensuring that visitors can enjoy a serene and spiritually enriching holiday. Additionally, the Maldives government has been actively promoting halal tourism, launching initiatives and campaigns to attract Muslim travelers from around the world.

Thaibah Pvt Ltd

Thaibah Hajj Umrah Pvt Ltd is an officially recognized travel agency in the Maldives, specializing in Hajj and Umrah pilgrimage services. Authorized by the Ministry of Islamic Affairs, it is among the 57 licensed companies permitted to organize Umrah pilgrimages for Maldivian citizens. The agency offers comprehensive pilgrimage packages, including visa assistance, flight bookings, accommodation, and transportation, ensuring a seamless and spiritually enriching experience for its clients. With a commitment to customer satisfaction and adherence to Islamic principles, Thaibah Hajj Umrah Pvt Ltd has established itself as a trusted name in the Maldives' pilgrimage travel sector.
